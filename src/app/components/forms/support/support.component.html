Support

If you experience any issues with the CasinoCoin wallet, there are multiple ways to contact the team:

Reddit: Join our official subreddit and post your question here. Discord: Live chat with the community and foundation here. Github: Report an error or bug on the CasinoCoin project site. FAQ: Go to the CasinoCoin FAQ page on the website. The answer may already be there. Email: Write to us at support@casinocoin.org and a support agent will get back to you. Social media: Like us on Facebook and follow us on Twitter. BitcoinTalk: Share your comments and ideas on whats latest @casinocoin, click here.
